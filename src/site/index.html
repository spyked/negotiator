SiAgent Automated Negotiator

Welcome to the SiAgent Automated Negotiator.

SiAgent wants to sell you a package consisting of some electronic components. You are able to negotiate the price with it. To begin a new negotiation, enter your name below, select the automated agent you wish to negotiate with (SiAgent or TFTAgent ) and click the New Session button.

SiAgent will propose an offer, and you can provide an answer by pressing one of the buttons in the interface. You can propose counter offers until round 5. If no agreement is made, the initial offer will be agreed upon. Also, you can leave the negotiation at any time by pressing the Opt out button. All the End round button does is delay the negotiation for a round. A price of 0 denotes that you do not wish to buy that object.

The negotiation will be logged for research purposes. To provide a comparison, it is recommended that you negotiate with the two agents (SiAgent and TFTAgent).

Note: The agent is still in an experimental stage. Bug reports and suggestions should go at lucian.mogosanu [at] gmail [dot] com .
